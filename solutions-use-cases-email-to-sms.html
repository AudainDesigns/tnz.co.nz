Email to SMS

Simplifying Email

Sending text messages is an effective way to connect with customers, but it can be cumbersome if you don't have the right tools. With the Email-to-SMS service, you can manage business texts from your email inbox.

Send an email containing your text message, and we'll deliver it to the mobile. Replies are sent back to your inbox. Simple.

The simplest way to stay in touch

Business SMS lets you contact customers instantly, wherever they are. Because users receive messages direct to their phones, they're less likely to miss or ignore them - most text messages are read and replied to within five minutes.

Faster workflow, more free time

Switching your communication to SMS frees up time spent sending individual messages, making phone calls and chasing up customer replies.

Insights through SMS Replies

Email to SMS includes built-in reporting options - you'll know who hasn't opened your messages, whether they reply and how long it takes - giving you valuable insight. Two-way communication means mobiles can reply to your message from their phone, and you'll receive the reply in your email inbox.

Works with most email clients

Send and receive texts with no special plugins or extra software. We support Outlook, Office365, Gmail, Thunderbird, iOS and Apple Mail, and most email clients.

SMS built into your software

When sending to New Zealand mobiles, your messages send from our shared short code numbers. We can optionally set you up with a dedicated short code to make it even easier for customers to text you first and start the conversation.

Extra Tools & Support Services

We make the Email-to-SMS learning curve easy, with step-by-step instructions and help from our support team. Plus a range of goodies, including the URL Shortener, web-based replies with Reply Link, sending files with File Link, calendar syncing for Calendar Reminder Automation, and more!

URL SHORTENER
SMS Reply Link and Enhanced SMS Replies

Use the built-in [[REPLY]] link to insert a short, clickable URL for web-based SMS Replies. SMS is a simple message format - only text with emojis allowed. The [[REPLY]] command adds a clickable link that mobiles can use to reply for free via the web.

REPLY LINK
Send files in SMS messages using File Link

Send pictures, videos, documents and files directly to mobiles for fast attention and engagement. All you need is the mobile number - no app download needed. Protect sensitive files with TLS encryption, PIN access and auto-expiring links. Capture attention with image thumbnails in text messages (uses Open Graph).

FILE LINK
Automated SMS, Email and Voice reminders

Send automated SMS, Email and Voice reminders using your Microsoft Outlook, Google or TNZ Calendar. Sync your existing calendar and generate reminders from your calendar's events reduces no-shows, late arrivals and rebookings with little change to the way you work.
